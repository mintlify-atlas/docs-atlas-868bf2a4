<div className="mt-16 mb-16 max-w-5xl mx-auto px-6">
  <h2 className="text-2xl sm:text-3xl font-semibold dark:text-white text-gray-900 mb-4">
    Quick Start
  </h2>
  <p className="text-base dark:text-gray-400 text-gray-600 mb-8">
    Get AtlasOS running on your Windows 11 system in minutes.
  </p>

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Check System Requirements">
      Ensure you're running Windows 11 (build 26100 or 26200). AtlasOS supports Windows 11 24H2 and 25H2 builds.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Download AME Wizard">
      Download the latest version of AME Wizard from [amelabs.net](https://amelabs.net) and the AtlasOS Playbook from the [GitHub releases page](https://github.com/Atlas-OS/Atlas/releases/latest).
    </Step>
    <Step title="Prepare Your System">
      Disable Windows Defender temporarily, ensure no pending Windows updates, and plug in your device to prevent interruptions during installation.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Run the Playbook">
      Open AME Wizard, load the AtlasOS Playbook (.apbx file), and follow the installation wizard. The process takes approximately 15 minutes.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Configure Post-Installation">
      After installation, access the Atlas folder on your desktop to customize security settings, performance options, and interface tweaks.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
